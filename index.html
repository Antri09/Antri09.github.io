Mini Maltese


Maltese are a small, long-haired dog breed. Their name is considered to have been taken from the island of Malta. Their origin is Italian. Their hair is white and long and their eyes are brown. They also do not pluck, except when it is their days when they have to be shorn. They don't need much exercise and are therefore considered ideal for apartments. Lifespan 12 to 15 years. Their weight is 3-4 kg. They are very friendly with children, they are not fussy, they are very cute and they do not grow much
